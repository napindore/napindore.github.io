Admissions

Eligibility requirement for allotting admission and accommodation :-

  1. Above 40 % disability necessary (Blindness)
  2. Family Income not more than 15000 per month.
  3. No caste criteria for admission.
  4. Only 25 seats available at Indore unit, 20 at Dakachya unit and 25 at Bhabra unit.
