About Us

National Assocation for the Blind is an organisation that works for the betterment of the Blind in the country. The Head Office of the organisation is in Mumbai. The organisation has 22 state branches and 133 district branches across the country.

Our Aims and Objects

The aims and objects for which NAB Indore is formed are :-

  1. To look after the welfare of the blind in the state generally.
  2. In particular, and without prejudice to the generality of the said premises :-
    • To amalgamate reconstruct and bring together various societies, Institutions, Establishments. Organizations and association and person or persons working for the uplift and welfare of the blind throughout state and to promote corporation and coordination amongst them.
    • To take steps to educate the blind in the State.
    • To establish or help in establishing Institution, workshops and homes for the education, rehabilitation, training, aftercare and welfare of the Blind in the State.
    • To take all necessary steps to organize and maintain employment agencies for getting the blind employed in trades, professions and occupations in the State.
    • To take such measures as may be necessary or expedient for training welfare workers, volunteers and administrators for carrying out in the State.
    • To establish and maintain libraries for the blind and to produce or promote the production of literature for the blind in all forms including braille, recordings on discs and tape, etc.
    • To conduct research and studies in the causes of blindness and to collect and collate statistics and concerning the blind in the State.
    • To look after the welfare of the dependents of the blind in the State.
    • To organize conferences, exhibitions, demonstrations and lectures to further the cause of the blind in the State.
    • To take all steps to mobiles public opinion to further the cause of the welfare of the blind in the State.
